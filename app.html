Tips & limits

Mind your quota. Each YouTube key gets 10,000 free units a day. One keyword uses roughly 100–300 units, so 2–4 broad searches a day is typical. Quota resets every morning.
Fewer keywords, deeper scan. 3–5 sharp keywords with a higher "scan per keyword" beats a long list of vague ones — and uses less quota.
"Both" is strict. The Long + Shorts filter only keeps channels whose recent uploads include both formats, so expect fewer results there.
Estimates, not guarantees. Voice language, content type and Facebook links are read from channel data — accurate most of the time, but not 100%. Always spot-check before outreach.
